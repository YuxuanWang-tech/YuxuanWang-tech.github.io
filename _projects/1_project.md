In my Ph.D. project, I model extreme thermonuclear runaways powered by unstable helium burning.

### Key Technical Highlights:
* **Implicit Hydrodynamics:** Used the **SHIVA code** to solve coupled non-linear PDEs under high-density regimes.
* **Data Pipelines:** Architected end-to-end **Python (Pandas, NumPy)** pipelines to parse and analyze TB-scale HPC simulation outputs.
* **Rare Event Prediction:** Mapped transition boundaries between stable burning and thermonuclear detonation, a process analogous to financial regime-switching detection.
